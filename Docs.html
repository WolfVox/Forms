How to Publish

Publishing on WolfVox is simple. Navigate to the "Publish Your Forms" button on the home page. You will be greeted with our Rich Text Editor.

Step 1: Enter Title > Step 2: Write Description > Step 3: Add Media > Step 4: Click Publish

Once published, your form is instantly visible to the community in the Forms dashboard.

About Plugins

Plugins are modular upgrades for your WolfVox experience. They do not cost real money but require a Human Verification check to install.

To get plugins, visit the Marketplace from the sub-navigation bar. Click "Get It" and type the randomized word in ALL CAPS to verify you are a developer and not a bot.

Chroma+ (More Colours)

By default, the editor allows one signature accent color. Unlocking Chroma+ provides a full palette of 6+ professional colors for your descriptions.

Select your text, click the palette icon, and choose your color. If the icon is highlighted with a purple border, it means your current selection is colored.

Expansion Pack (Slots)

The standard WolfVox form allows 1 image, 1 video, and 1 link. The Expansion Pack unlocks the [+] buttons.

With this plugin, you can add an unlimited number of URLs and assets to a single form, making your posts more detailed and professional.

Terms & Conditions

By using WolfVox, you agree that you are responsible for the content you post. We do not store private data other than what you explicitly provide in the forms.

Warning: Any attempt to bypass the Human Verification system or exploit the API will result in a permanent hardware ban.

Content Rules

WolfVox is a professional developer community. Please follow these rules:

  • No Bad Words: Profanity and offensive language are strictly prohibited.
  • Respect: Do not post content intended to harass others.
  • Quality: Ensure your links and images are working before publishing.

Forms reported for "Bad Language" or "Spam" will be reviewed by the WolfVox automated system and removed if they violate these rules.
